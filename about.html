MY JOURNEY TO MALAYSIA

Hello, my name is Harish Chandolu. I am from India and currently pursuing my Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science Engineering (CSE – Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning) at Vel Tech Rangarajan Dr. Sagunthala R&D Institute of Science and Technology, Chennai.

My visit to Malaysia was a very special experience because it was my first international travel experience. During my semester exchange program, I had the opportunity to explore the rich culture, traditions, heritage sites, and diverse lifestyle of Malaysia.

Through this website, I am sharing my first travel blog where I document the cultural destinations, famous festivals, traditional food, and unique experiences I discovered during my journey across Malaysia. From the colorful steps of Batu Caves to the historic streets of George Town and the cultural beauty of Malacca, each destination reflects the diversity and harmony of Malaysian culture.

“Travel is not only about visiting new places, but also about learning new cultures, meeting new people, and creating memories that last forever.”
